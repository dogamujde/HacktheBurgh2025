Learning Outcomes
On completion of this course, the student will be able to:
  1. Demonstrate verbally and in writing a broad knowledge of the work and career of Pablo Picasso in a variety of media.
  2. Identify the relationships between Picasso's career and works to that of artists and others who worked in the same period.
  3. Present their ideas clearly and succinctly to their peers using both oral and visual means of communication.
  4. Conduct independent library research in the field of modern art in support of written work.
  5. Engage with original works of art by Picasso and his peers first hand and have considered what can be learnt from this experience.
